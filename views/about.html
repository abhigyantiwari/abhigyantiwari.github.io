The Story

Born on 1998, December 17, used to broke everything came into my way. Be it a Generator room for a factory or RC car. No kid can have the amount of curiosity I have then to know how things work. The interests for machines grew year by year massively.

In 2011, father gifted first computer. In the next two years, the interests for machines and cars increased even more via internet. Due to that 'lan cable', later, interests started to shift to coding and how computers worked. I did a mistake of deep diving in computer world that I got lost in it and didn't clear my high school.

Today, I can make both Front-end and Back-end parts of a Website/Web-application. There comes a joy, more than anybody, when I craft Web-pages/Apps (CSS suck though); Be it a database-driven Web-aplication or a static website. I also like designing before developing the projects: on paper and then digitally, keeping typography, colors and your content? in mind.
